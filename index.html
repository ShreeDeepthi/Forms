An Archive of Almost's

Step 1 of 9

What should we call you?

You can use your name or a pen name. This is how your story will be credited.

What's your email address?

We'll only use this to reach out if your story is selected.

Where are you from?

A village, a town, a city, a neighbourhood — wherever feels like home.

What would you like to share?

A memory, a ritual, a recipe, a dialect, a person, an almost-forgotten place.

Why is this story important to you?

Help us understand what this memory means, and why it deserves to be kept.

Is this story connected to a specific place?

A house, a street, a temple, a river bend — anything we could one day visit.

Is there someone we should speak to?

If someone else knows this story well, tell us who — and how to reach them.

Do you have photographs you'd like to share?

One photograph is enough. Max 10 MB — JPG, PNG, or HEIC.

Can we feature your story?

With your permission, we may include this in a future publication or the public archive.

Thank you for trusting us with this.

Your story has been added to the archive. If it's chosen for a future publication, we'll write to the email you shared.

Thank you for helping us preserve the memories that matter.
